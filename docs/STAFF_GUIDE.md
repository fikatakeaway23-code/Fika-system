# Staff Guide — Fika Takeaway App

This guide is for **Barista 1** (Morning) and **Barista 2** (Afternoon).

---

## Logging In

1. Open the Fika app on the tablet or your phone
2. Tap your profile card — **Barista 1** or **Barista 2**
3. Enter your 4-digit PIN
4. You're in

**Default PINs** (change these after first login):
- Barista 1: `1111`
- Barista 2: `2222`

> **Lost your PIN?** Ask the owner — they can reset it from Settings.

---

## Starting Your Shift Report

1. Tap **Start Shift Report** on the Home screen
2. The app creates a draft and saves it locally — you won't lose progress if you close the app
3. Work through the 7 steps at your own pace

---

## The 7 Steps

### Step 1 — Opening Checklist
- Tick each item as you complete it
- Enter the **Opening Float** (cash in the drawer at open)
- Optional: take a photo of the clean counter setup

### Step 2 — Espresso Dial-in
After pulling your calibration shot, enter:
- **Dose** — how many grams of coffee in the portafilter (target ~18g)
- **Yield** — how many grams in the cup (target ~36g)
- **Extraction Time** — seconds (target 25–30 sec)
- **Taste** — Sour / Balanced / Bitter / Flat

The app will give you a tip if the shot needs adjustment.

### Step 3 — Inventory
Record opening and closing stock for:
- Beans (grams)
- Milk (litres)
- Ice cream, cups, lids, bakery items
- Toggle Syrups OK / Boba OK / Straws OK

### Step 4 — Cash Log
Enter:
- **Opening Float** — cash in drawer at start (same as Step 1)
- **Cash Sales** — total cash collected from customers
- **Digital Sales** — eSewa / QR / card total
- **Closing Cash** — cash in drawer at end of shift

The app shows if there's a discrepancy over NPR 50.

### Step 5 — Waste Log
Record honestly:
- Calibration shots pulled
- Milk wasted (ml)
- Drinks remade
- Unsold pastries
- Any notes on why waste happened

### Step 6 — Closing Checklist
Tick each cleaning and closing task. Take a closing photo if you like.

### Step 7 — Issues & Notes
- Total drinks served (required)
- Pastries sold
- Most popular drink of the day
- Any equipment issues (toggle on and describe)
- Any customer complaints
- General shift notes for the owner or next barista

---

## Submitting Your Report

Tap **Submit Report ✓** on Step 7.

A confirmation dialog appears — once submitted, the report is locked.

> **If you're offline:** The app saves your draft. Submit when you're back on Wi-Fi.

---

## Logging Expenses

Tap the **Expense** tab at the bottom.

Fill in:
- Name (e.g. "Milk from supplier")
- Amount in NPR
- Category
- Who paid

The expense goes to the owner's review queue immediately.

---

## Your Profile

Tap the **Profile** tab to:
- See your shift hours
- Change your PIN (enter current PIN first)
- Log out

---

## Notifications

The app sends a reminder 10 minutes before your shift:
- Barista 1: 5:50 AM
- Barista 2: 11:50 AM

Make sure notifications are allowed in your phone settings.

---

## Tips

- **Don't skip the espresso dial-in** — it takes 2 minutes and makes every cup better
- **Record waste honestly** — the owner uses this to improve operations, not to penalise
- **Cash discrepancies happen** — enter the actual numbers, the app flags it and the owner investigates
- **Use shift notes** — write anything useful for the next barista or for the owner

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| App shows "Offline" | Check Wi-Fi. Draft is saved — submit when online |
| Wrong PIN | Wait 1 minute, try again. Ask owner to reset if needed |
| Report disappeared | Go to Home → tap "Continue Report" — it's saved as a draft |
| Can't submit | Make sure Step 7 has "Total drinks served" filled in |
